    What are custom patches?

    Custom patches are personalized designs crafted through embroidery, weaving, or printing, tailored to showcase logos, messages, or unique artwork. These versatile patches are often applied to clothing, hats, bags, and other accessories. Organizations like businesses, military units, sports teams, and law enforcement use them to promote brands, signify memberships, or represent affiliations.

    Are patches washable?

    Yes, patches are generally washable, but durability depends on the materials and backing used. To maximize longevity, wash garments inside out on a gentle cycle with cold or lukewarm water, avoiding bleach and fabric softeners. Our patches withstand up to 50 wash cycles when cared for properly.
